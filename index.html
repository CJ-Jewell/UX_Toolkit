The Design Process

This text will include our mission statement.

The design thinking process is iterative, flexible, and focused on collaboration between designers and users.

The prcess is nonlinear. It folds back on itself and jumps around the process while solving human-centered problems. Use this process when you want to create a new product or service, or to improve a product or service that already exists.

The design thinking process can transform the way organizations develop products, services, processes, and strategies. It can develop people's creative potential, and can spur innovation.

The first phase of the design thinking process exists to gain an empathic understanding of the problem you're trying to solve.
  • Learn about the users
  • Learn about the company
  • Learn about the competition

In the second phase of the design thinking process, you put together the research you have created and gathered during the empathize phase.
  • Analyze your observations
  • Synthesize research to define the core problems
  • Identify a problem and user to focus on
